PERFECT SKINCARE ROUTINE

Skincare Routine, Nov 27, 2020

The morning routine is all about protection from the sun, pollution and the elements.As your skin naturally repairs itself at night, your nighttime routine should be all about treatment and giving your skin what it needs.It’s not enough to have good skin-care products: For your products to be most effective, you also need to apply them in the correct order. Your routine will depend on your skin type, the ingredients and formulations of your products, and the time of day. A good rule of thumb, though, is to apply in order of texture, from thinnest to thickest, since thin products won’t be able to penetrate thicker ones. What order you apply your skincare products in matters almost as much as what you’re putting on your face. Apply certain products in the wrong order, and they could be rendered ineffective or simply not work as well.

Step 1: Cleanser
Step 2: Toner
Step 3: Serum
Step 4: Spot Treatment
Step 5: Eye Cream
Step 6: Moisturizer
Step 7: Face Oil
Final Step: Sunscreen (AM Only)


DO YOU KNOWS TONERS HELP

Benefit toners, Nov 28, 2020

Toners are most helpful and necessary for people who want extra cleansing after wearing makeup or other heavy skin product such as sunscreen. Applying a small amount of toner to a soft cotton ball or pad and gently blotting and wiping your face with it will remove oil and give the appearance of smaller pores.Our skin is naturally acidic,typically with a pH balance of between five and six (on a scale from 0 to 14).Toners can help close pores and tighten cell gaps after cleasning,reducing the penetration of impuririties and environmental contaminants into the skin.Toners also can help to bind moisture to the skin .
